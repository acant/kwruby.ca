Getting Started with Ruby

Some resources for getting start with Ruby have been mentioned either during meetings or by members:

  • Getting Started with Rails - Rails Guides
  • Ruby on Rails Tutorial by Michael Hartl
  • exercism.io, a service to trying and learning a variety of languages including Ruby
  • Eloquent Ruby by Russ Olsen
  • Ruby Rogues podcast
  • Bike Shed podcast
  • Contestoga College has a continuing education course PROG1190 - Ruby I
  • Ruby Rails Link has a slack channel and a nice wiki with lots of useful resources
  • Learn Ruby on Rails from Thoughtbot

Leigh Halliday also maintains an extensive list of Ruby resources https://www.leighhalliday.com/best-ruby-and-ruby-on-rails-resources
